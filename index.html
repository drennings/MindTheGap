So you want to do GSE?

Are you part of a globally distributed software engineering project? Or trying to learn how to manage one? Or maybe you didn’t even start such a project, but are looking to engage in one and want to know how to deal with the challenges of a globally distributed software engineering project. Then you came to the right place! More and more companies are using globally distributed software engineering as a tool for their software development process. On this website, we speak of Globally distributed software engineering as a contractual relationship between a client and vendor organization, where the client organization outsources parts of its development process. There are a number of advantages of globally distributed software engineering which are stated in this paper. This website will focus mainly on the solutions of the disadvantages, which is why this website is setup by a group of students from the Technical University of Delft. We are trying to propose valuable solutions to four core challenges which pose serious risks to the smooth execution of a globally distributed software engineering project. These four core challenges are communication, coordination, cultural differences and team bonding. In the next section a “skillmap” is defined for these four challenges. Per challenge a number of sub challenges are defined and per sub challenge solutions are proposed. These can be accessed by hovering your mouse through the “skillmap”.
